This guide walks you through your specific situation step by step. Answer a few quick questions, tick through a short checklist, and you’ll arrive at a ready-to-send email. The whole thing takes minutes.
Do

Don’t

Send a government notice through the normal flow
Escalate without completing this checklist first
Who is this for?
Vendor and Workforce Managers, Project and Programme Managers, Community Support Team Leads, and Operations staff — anyone at Oneforma who may need to initiate a legal escalation.

Step 2 of 4
Any fraud indicators in this case?
This includes: suspected fraud, ToS violations, account anomalies, or non-payment tied to work quality concerns — even if the primary case type is not fraud.
If unsure, choose yes. The Fraud team will confirm whether their involvement is needed. Escalating to Legal without Fraud team input where fraud is suspected is one of the most common reasons escalations are returned.
⚠
Yes — there are fraud or suspicious activity indicators
The Fraud pre-check will be added as the first step in your checklist
✓
No — no fraud indicators, straightforward case
Proceed directly to the checklist
